Lucky One mall is the largest mall in Pakistan and South Asia with an area of 3.4 million sq. feet. Which is owned by Yunus Brothers Group. It's construction was started in 2010, and was opened in 2017. The Dining options at LuckyOne Mall will take your taste buds to storm, from Local favorites to renowned international fast food joints, continental, oriental, Chinese, Thai & Mediterranean cuisines. And on ground, first and second floors, a wide variety of brands are also presents with different items of wonderful tastes! Besides this, A very large food court is also situated on the 2nd floor of the mall with Onderland, the largest indoor entertaining place.
